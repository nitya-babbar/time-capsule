Remember 3 Words

Rules

You will be given 3 words to remember. You will then have to play a tic-tac-toe game. Once the game is over, you will be asked to type in the 3 words again. You will have five chances to get them correct, so be careful!

To start, click "Get 3 Words" to receive your words. If you would like new words, click "New Words". Once you have analyzed your words, click "Done Looking at Words" to play tic-tac-toe. Once the game is over, you may select "Done Tic-Tac-Toe" to attempt to re-enter the words given to you. If you would like to play again, or restart, you may then click the restart button.
