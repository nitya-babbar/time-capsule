Memory Match Game

Rules

You will be given 16 cards faced down. You can click on a card to flip it over. Once one card is flipped over, you will be allowed to flip over another card. If the two cards facing up are the same, they will remain face up on the board. If they are different, both cards will be flipped back over, and you can try again. The goal is to find all 8 pairs of matching cards, and the game is over once all cards are face up.

You may start by clicking the "start" button. If you would like to play again, or restart, you may then click the "restart" button.
