Anagrams Game

Rules

You will be given a set of letters and 60 seconds to make as many 3+ letter words as possible. You will get 1 point for 3-letter words, 2 points for 4-letter words, 3 points for 5-letter words, and so on. Do your best to make as many words as you can in the time given!

You can start by selecting a level, and then clicking "start". Try to type as many words as possible, until the time runs out. If you would like to shuffle the letters given, click the "shuffle" button in the blue box. Your score is displayed in the orange box. If you would like to play again, or restart, you may then click the "restart" button.
