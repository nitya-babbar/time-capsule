What is the Journal?

You can use the journal to keep track of your daily ideas and routines. Everyday, come here and write what you did so that you can read back on it in the later days.

Click "open" to open your journal, see what you did earlier in the week, and begin to write. Click "close" when you are done journaling. Click "save" along the way to save your work.
